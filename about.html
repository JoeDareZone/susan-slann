About

Susan Weaver

Norwich, UK

Susan Weaver works from Nutmeg studios in Little Fransham. Her work is varied and experimental, as she constantly strives to discover new ways to express herself and explore the fundamental emotions that connect us all.

Susan has always been fascinated by the relationship between paint or materials and how their interaction resonates with our senses and emotions. As a painter and printmaker, the physical process of transformation plays a significant role in her creative practice, reflecting her ongoing curiosity and passion for artistic exploration.
